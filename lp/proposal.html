The build · 04 / 10

How we install it, from first call to a live tool.

A clear sequence of phases so you always know what is happening and what comes next.

Phase 01

Discovery and process audit

We sit with you and document the one workflow this tool will own: the steps, the inputs, the rules and the people involved.

Phase 02

Architecture and modeling

We turn your SOPs into a data model and a flow: what gets entered, what gets calculated, what gets produced.

Phase 03

Build and branding

We build the working tool with your catalog, pricing, terms and branding baked in.

Phase 04

Integration and data

We connect it to the tools and data you already use, so it fits your stack instead of adding another silo.

Phase 05

Testing on live work

You run it on real jobs. We fix, tune and refine against what actually happens day to day.

Phase 06

Handover and training

We hand over the tool, the access and a short walkthrough so your team is running it confidently from day one.

What we manage after handover

  • Optional support if you want us on call for changes as your business grows.
  • Enhancements scoped as small follow-on builds when you need new capability.
  • No lock-in. The tool is yours whether or not you keep us involved.
Under the hood · 05 / 10

Built on your data, fit to your stack, owned by you.
