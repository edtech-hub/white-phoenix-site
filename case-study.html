About the client

WeddingClickz Photography

WeddingClickz is a Bengaluru photography and videography studio that shoots weddings, pre-wedding stories and cinematic films for couples across India. Their pitch is refreshingly un-corporate — every wedding is a story waiting to be told, and they tell it with real warmth.

The work was landing. The admin around it wasn't. As enquiries climbed, the studio's quoting — done by hand, one couple at a time — quietly became the thing standing between interest and a signed booking.

The Challenge

What was holding them back

Picture a typical enquiry. A couple falls for the portfolio, messages to ask about pricing, and then waits. Someone opens Canva, builds a quote by hand around their events and coverage, exports it, and emails it back — often a day or two later. By then the couple has usually asked three other studios too.

  • !Every quote was designed by hand in Canva — slow, and hard to keep consistent
  • !Turnaround ran to one or two days, while couples compared studios in real time
  • !Pricing shifted with events, coverage and add-ons, so manual quotes were easy to get wrong
  • !Enquiry details lived in scattered chats, with no tidy record of who asked for what
  • !Evenings went into building quotes instead of editing the work that actually wins clients
Our Solution

How we solved it

We replaced the Canva-and-wait routine with a tool that does the quoting itself. Couples walk through three simple steps, and the moment they finish, a clean quote lands in their inbox — no human in the loop, no overnight delay.

  • ✓A three-step flow couples actually finish: pick events, choose photo and video coverage, get the price
  • ✓Pricing logic baked in, so every combination of events and add-ons comes out right, every time
  • ✓Quotes generated automatically and emailed straight to the couple — no manual design, no copy-paste
  • ✓A built-in date picker, email validation and reCAPTCHA to keep enquiries clean and the spam out
  • ✓Every quote captured with the couple's dates and contact details, so nothing slips through the cracks
Our Process

How we delivered it

01

Discovery & Alignment

We sat down with the studio's real packages and the back-and-forth they have with couples every week, and turned all of it into rules the tool could follow.

02

Pricing Logic

Events, coverage tiers, add-ons — we mapped exactly how the price builds up, so the engine quotes the way the owner would, not a watered-down version of it.

03

UX & Workflow Design

We kept the flow to three plain-English steps. No jargon, nothing to overthink — just the few choices that actually move the price.

04

Development

We built the configurator, the pricing engine and the automated email quote, then wired in validation and reCAPTCHA so the inbox stays usable.

05

Testing & Validation

We ran every realistic combination of events and coverage against the studio's own numbers until the quotes matched, every single time.

06

Deployment & Optimisation

We shipped it in January 2026 at weddingclickzquotation.com and have kept tightening the flow since, so more couples reach a price without dropping off.
